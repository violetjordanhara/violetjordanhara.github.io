Violet Jordan Hara

Vi is a fiction writer. Her work is published under her birth name Erika Loh until March 2021 when she changed her name legally to Violet Jordan Hara. She is a final year student at Yale-NUS College, an independent liberal arts college jointly founded by Yale and NUS until the latter decided to shut the institution down overnight. She will graduate in 2022 with a Literature degree and a minor in Global Antiquity.
